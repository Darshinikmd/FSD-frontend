About the tour:

Make this trip a dream come true as you visit some of the most happening and popular destinations in Europe. You start weaving your story of Europe with the city of
Rome, a place famous for its gastronomical delicacies and rich history and culture. In Rome you witness one of the wonders of the world, the Colosseum, after Rome
you carry on with your expedition of Europe to Florence which is home to the great Leonardo Da Vinci. In Florence get a chance to visit another wonder of the world,
The Leaning Tower of Pisa. After which you get ready to unravel the beauty of Switzerland. When you venture out for Mount Titlis on Rotair, you will step into the
world of Narnia to which Lucy opened the wardrobe door. Witness the Rhine Falls and feel its beastly energy causing an illusion of oozing clouds. A trip to Europe
is not complete without fancy souvenirs of the places you visit, so do make sure you bring home lots of memorabilia along with happy memories.
